    I draw when I feel overwhelmed by life. Usually I write a text on the side, as it helps me sort out thoughts and worries.

    I ran a drawing class in Henningsvær, Lofoten. Here, I have noticed that drawing has a meditative effect on people. I believe everyone can draw, it is just a matter of perspective and patience.
