  • Whereabouts

    • Guro, 26 y/o. Always on the look for the reflection of the sun. Know how to code and how to find solutions. Have a deep passion for coffee, collages and hikes. And doodling.

    I have a bachelor degree from University of Oslo. This one. Here, I studied interaction design and did my equivalient to a minor in social anthropology. In social anthropology, I took classes that emphasised our global troubles (Overheating & the linear idea of development). My field of interest is Sustainable Design, and I find the Transition Design Methodology very intriguing.
    I did a research study during my bachelor, on a Community of Practice on a online help desk for the users of our worlds biggest free and open source health information software (Dhis2). In January 2023 I travelled to Malawi with the same group of researchers. We presented our work at the DSA2023 Conference in Reading, England.

    Since August 2021 I have been living my life as a cafemanager – included doing some graphical design, drawing classes, one musical performance, event management and hiring – at Trevarefabrikken in Henningsvær, Lofoten. This is also where you find me Spring 2023.



    I am currently living in my converted VW Transporter (T5). Contact me by calling +47 406 28 219. Or email me at guro@handeland.info
